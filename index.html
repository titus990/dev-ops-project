About Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport in which two people, usually wearing protective gloves and other protective equipment such as hand wraps and mouthguards, throw punches at each other for a predetermined amount of time in a boxing ring.

Known as "The Sweet Science", boxing has evolved from its ancient origins to become a refined sport of strategy, skill, and athleticism. Modern boxing emphasizes footwork, defensive techniques, and precise punching.

At Knockout Boxing, we honor the traditions of the sport while incorporating modern training methods to develop complete fighters who excel both in the ring and in life.
